Features

Custom Keyboard Shortcuts

Create your own shortcuts with any modifier combination. Press-to-record makes capturing shortcuts effortless.

Multi-Engine Support

Works seamlessly on Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, and Yahoo. Your shortcuts follow you everywhere.

Instant Refinement

Append any text to your current search query. Filter by site, add keywords, or customize results instantly.

Full Control

Enable, disable, edit, or delete shortcuts anytime. Warnings for browser shortcut conflicts keep you informed.

How to Use

1

Search for anything

Search on Google, Bing, DuckDuckGo, or Yahoo. Get the usual mix of results.

2

Want real opinions? Press your shortcut

Looking at generic SEO content but want to know what actual people think? Press ⌥R and your results instantly filter to Reddit only.

3

Add more shortcuts

Click the extension icon to create shortcuts for Stack Overflow, GitHub, Hacker News, or any site you frequently want to filter to.

Example Shortcuts

⌥ R Filter to Reddit
⌥ S Filter to Stack Overflow
⌥ G Filter to GitHub
⌥ H Filter to Hacker News
